How It Works

The Couch to Kili programme

Your journey broken into three phases. Each one builds on the last, so by the time you're on the mountain, the hard work is already done.

01

Months 1–2

Build your foundation

We start where you are. Whether you walk to work or run marathons, your plan is tailored to your starting point. No one gets left behind.

  • Personalised training plan delivered to your inbox
  • Group video call — meet your team
  • Full gear guide with budget alternatives
02

Months 3–4

Build your confidence

The training gets harder. The bond with your group gets stronger. This is where most people surprise themselves.

  • UK training hike — Snowdon or Scafell Pike
  • Altitude sickness briefing with a specialist
  • One-to-one call with your expedition leader
  • Summit buddy pairing
03

Months 5–6 + Expedition

Summit ready

Final preparation, then Tanzania. Eight days on the mountain with daily health checks, your group, and your expedition leader.

  • Pre-departure information session
  • Airport transfers + hotel in Moshi
  • Expedition medic on the mountain
  • 8-day Lemosho/Rongai route climb
  • Summit certificate + celebration event
