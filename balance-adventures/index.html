How It Works

The Couch to Kili
Programme

Your 12-week journey to Africa's highest summit, broken into three distinct phases. Each one builds on the last.

01

Weeks 1 – 4

Build Your Foundation

We start exactly where you are. Whether you're a walker, runner, or gym-goer, your training plan is tailored to your starting point. No one gets left behind.

  • Personalised training plan delivered by email
  • Group video call — meet your team
  • WhatsApp cohort group goes live
  • Full gear guide with budget alternatives
  • Weekly voice note from Will every Monday
02

Weeks 5 – 8

Build Your Confidence

The training gets harder. So does the bond between you and your group. This is where most people surprise themselves.

  • UK training hike — Snowdon or Scafell Pike
  • Altitude sickness briefing with a specialist
  • One-to-one video call with Will
  • Fortnightly group check-in calls
  • Summit buddy pairing
03

Weeks 9 – 12 + Expedition

Summit Ready

Final preparation, then Tanzania. Seven days on the mountain with daily health checks, your group around you, and Will leading the way.

  • Pre-departure information session
  • Airport transfers and hotel in Moshi
  • Expedition doctor on the mountain
  • 7-day Machame Route climb
  • Summit certificate + celebration call
